About Event

The City of Ekurhuleni is hosting its inaugural Public Private Partnership (PPP) investor summit on 20-21 November 2018 at Emperors Palace, Kempton Park, and Gauteng. The EKURHULENI PPP INVESTOR SUMMIT 2018 - Showcasing the City’s PPP roll-out plans is inspired by a desire to see Ekurhuleni taking the lead in economic development, growth and sustainability on the entire continent.

The summit will see the convergence of successful PPP stakeholders from public and private sectors ranging from leading developers, builders, architects, financial institutions, consultants to attorneys and many more, who will share insights into working long-term PPPs that lead to economic development, growth and sustainability.

The City of Ekurhuleni’s economy being considered a primary driver of economic growth in Gauteng and favorably located in the core economic activity and employment catchment aims to shape a new identity based on futuristic principles of urban design and lead Africa’s new industrial revolution.

To this end, the summit is very much in line with the Metropolitan Spatial Development Framework (MSDF) which dovetails with the overarching vision of the city, encapsulated in the Growth and Development Strategy 2055 (GDS 2055), thus the strategic imperative Re-urbanise - to achieve sustainable urban integration. To take advantage of this first of its kind in the City of Ekurhuleni, you are cordially invited be a part of our world class event by becoming a partner and/or event partner to live, play and invest in the campaign.
