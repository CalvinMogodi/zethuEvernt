About Event

The aim of the City of Ekurhuleni’s PPP Investor Summit is to officially launch the proposed PPP developments and showcase the PPP project proposals, which are part of the City’s Re-Urbanise strategic objective as informed by the 2055 Growth and Development Strategy (GDS 2055). The summit is intended to stimulate the private investor community as well as demonstrate the City’s readiness to engage private investors and other relevant stakeholders in ensuring a successful implementation of the proposed PPP developments.

The central theme of the Summit is showcasing the City’s PPP roll-out plans, which are managed by Real Estate Department, as part of their strategic function to guide the City in creating value through the property portfolio and improve the management of the portfolio. The Summit must also provide a platform to communicate the City’s broader vision and its investment facilitation message to the markets while also showcasing other catalytic projects and mega infrastructure programmes driven as part of the City’s ‘come invest’ campaign.

Business will learn how to Partner with the City of Ekurhuleni.
The Ekurhuleni PPP conference is the largest exhibition event in the City for creating new public private partnerships in the real estate industry. It is a must attend event for industry executives and champions looking to develop relationships with local and state governments as well to network with other global players. With the national real estate industry in attendance, municipal officials from all over the country are attending conference to unveil the City of Ekurhuleni’s development goals, redevelopment visions, and prospective projects.

Education: Expand your Public Private Partnership Knowledge.
Led by the industry’s brightest talent, the conference will feature in-depth discussions on the latest topics, emerging trends and expanding roles of public private partnerships specific to the City of Ekurhuleni. Sessions are designed to inform attendees on how the City is transforming spaces within the municipality into economically vibrant communities and a great deal of attention on how the City plans to improve the livelihood of local communities. Industry experts will present practical financing techniques and private public partnership approaches that can be used for your next development projects in the city.
